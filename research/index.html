    experiments in offbeat music videos using lucid sonic dreams and demucs

  • generating audio with LSTMs

    using lstms to make experimental game music, for indie developers and their very limited budgets

  • compositional pattern producing networks

    asking compositional pattern producing networks to take a turn at album artwork
