hello, i'm aarav pillai (he/him)

i work at the intersection of creative machine learning, healthcare ai, and internet governance. my research is less interested in spectacle than in consequence; what systems quietly assume, how they reshape practice once deployed, and which values become infrastructural through repetition.

i currently lead research at qinārā ai lab, where we build experimental creative systems while critically examining the power structures embedded within technical architectures. i previously worked as an associate lecturer at the ual creative computing institute, supporting postgraduate teaching across the msc data science & ai for the creative industries and ma/msc computing & creative industry programmes.

before this, i also worked as an independent data scientist on public-sector and humanitarian projects. i am now preparing for doctoral research in computer science; a period marked by several annotated papers, evolving research questions, and too many open browser tabs.
