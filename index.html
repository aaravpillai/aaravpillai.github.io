hello, i'm aarav pillai (he/him)

i build and study systems at the crossroads of creative ml, healthcare ai, accessibility, and internet governance. i care less about spectacle and more about infrastructure; what assumptions harden into defaults, what behaviours get nudged into normality, and how values sediment into code.

i currently lead research at qinārā ai lab, designing experimental systems while interrogating the politics embedded inside them. before that, i taught at the ual creative computing institute; training students to both ship systems and side-eye them.
