this section gathers writing in its loosest sense: notes, fragments, reviews, and occasional longer pieces that function less as publication and more as record. i use my goodreads and letterboxd as public marginalia; places to think alongside books and films while they are still warm. my blogs serve as a slower diary and a method of curation, documenting interests as they shift rather than resolving them into arguments. some fiction and unfinished work also lives on wattpad, where writing is allowed to be provisional, excessive, or quietly abandoned. taken together, these spaces form a personal archive rather than a portfolio; a way of noticing what keeps returning.
